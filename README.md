# Azuresong

*A modded Vintage Story server focused on immersion, community, and the growth of player-driven civilizations.*

## About

Azuresong is a heavily modded Vintage Story server designed to create a world where players can progress beyond simple homesteads and villages. The long-term vision is to foster the development of settlements into thriving towns and eventually true cities, complete with infrastructure, industries, territories, and specialized roles within the community.

Whether you prefer farming, mining, blacksmithing, construction, trade, exploration, governance, or storytelling, your contributions can help shape the future of the world.

## Server Goals

The core objective of Azuresong is to create a living world where:

* Small homesteads grow into villages.
* Villages develop into towns and cities.
* Players establish industries and trade networks.
* Settlements develop unique identities and cultures.
* Territories emerge through exploration and expansion.
* Communities create their own stories, laws, and traditions.
* Specialized professions and player-driven jobs become meaningful parts of society.

Rather than focusing solely on individual progression, Azuresong encourages players to build something larger than themselves.

## Features

* Curated modpack focused on immersion and world-building.
* Enhanced crafting, survival, and progression systems.
* Encouragement of roleplay and community interaction.
* Long-term settlement and infrastructure development.
* Exploration-driven gameplay.
* Player-driven economies and trade opportunities.
* Collaborative projects and large-scale construction.

## Roleplay

Roleplay is encouraged but not required.

Players are welcome to immerse themselves in their characters, settlements, professions, and local communities. The server is designed to support organic storytelling through gameplay rather than requiring strict roleplay rules.

## Vision

The world of Azuresong is intended to evolve through the actions of its players. Every road, workshop, farm, mine, marketplace, district, and city should exist because players chose to build it.

The greatest accomplishments on the server should not be individual possessions, but the communities, settlements, and civilizations that players create together.
